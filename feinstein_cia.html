March 11, 2014, Senator Diane Feinstein presents evidence of unconstitutional CIA on the floor of the Senate.

On March 11, Senator Feinstein took to the floor of the Senate to denounce CIA surveillance of the Senate Select Committees investigation of torture in the CIA's Rendition, Detention and Interrogation program. The CIA has monitored the files access by Senate Staffers, deceived investigators and intimidated their overseers.

The techniques used against the Senate Committee by the CIA are applied to every American by the NSA. Our Internet usage is logged, NSA officials deceive the American people and whistle blowers are intimidated with spurious Espionage act charges.

A culture of deception and lawlessness has run rampant in the in the United States of America's Intelligence agencies during Diane Feinstein's tenure as Chair of the Select Committee. It is time to bring all unconstitutional surveillance to an end.
